ABOUT ME

Born in 2005 in Colombia, Honda-Tolima.
I am convinced that web design can be more
inclusive and exciting. My purpose is to discover
how to unite technology and design to create digital
experiences that are not only attractive, but also
functional and accessible to all users. In my free
time I really like to play video games and listen to
music. I think that music has the power to connect
emotions and tell stories without the need for words,
which inspires me in my creative process. I also enjoy
exploring new tools and learning techniques that will
help me improve my programming and design skills.
My goal is to continue growing in the world of
technology, always looking for ways to innovate
and make digital design more inclusive for everyone.
