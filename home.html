About Me

Born in 2005 in Colombia, Honda-Tolima. I am convinced that web design can be more inclusive and exciting. While learning to program, I seek to explore new forms of expression through experimentation and creativity. My purpose is to discover how to unite technology and design to create digital experiences that are not only attractive, but also functional and accessible to all users.
